Magic Items

Orcus Skull

Legendary artifact, requires attunement by an evil creature.

While you are attuned to the skull, it hovers around your head, flitting in and out of existence as you cast your spells. You may increase either your Charisma or your Intelligence by 4, to a maximum of 24. You become immune to poison. When you reduce an enemy to 0 hit points it must roll a Constitution saving throw or return as a zombie or skeleton under your command instantly. You may control a number of skeletons or zombies in this way equal to your Charisma modifier.

You learn the chill touch cantrip if you did not already know it. The skull knows the following spells:

  • Inflict Wounds
  • Blindness/ Deafness
  • Animate Dead
  • Blight
  • Contagion
  • Finger of Death
  • Abi-Dalzim's Horrid Wilting

Additionally, the skull grants you the ability to consume one of your undead followers to bolster your own defenses. As a reaction, if an undead creature you control is within 5 feet of you, you may sacrifice that undead (which obliterates the corpse) to create a magical set of bone plate armor around you that increases your AC by 7 until the start of your next turn.
