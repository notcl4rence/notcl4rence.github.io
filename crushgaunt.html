The Corpse Taker's Crush Gaunt

Wondrous item, very rare.

This gauntlet is made of black metal mined from the Abyss itself, and looks as though it is part of a larger set of armor. The back of the hand is molded into the shape of a skull, and when it is picked up green fire fills the eyes and lines the skull in the shape of the sigil of necromancy. The metal encasing the fingers is twisted and tapers to claws at the fingertips. The metal itself is pitted and stained with old blood, and rust and gore cause the joints to creak with every minute movement.

While you are attuned to this gauntlet, you gain the following benefits:

  • Undead under your control may add their proficiency bonus to their damage rolls and their AC, and they use your spell attack bonus when rolling to hit with their attacks.
  • The maximum hit points of any undead you raise increases by 10.
  • Attacks by your undead count as magical for the purposes of overcoming resistance.
  • You may animate small animals with CR 1/8 or less without expending materials or a spell slot.
  • A number of times per long rest equal to your Charisma modifier, you may flense the flesh from any humanoid corpse or small animal, leaving a perfectly cleaned skeleton
